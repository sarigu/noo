about noo.

We are noo. We create delicious homemade dumplings. Visit us in our casual restaurant in Copenhagen, bridging Chinese street culture with Scandinavian simplicity or take some tasty dumplings home with you. We’re on a mission to make great tasting dumplings available to as many people as possible.
